More about me

I’m Ryan McCaulsky— a product designer based in New York City. My current focus is establishing visual identity and product prototypes for startups based in underserved communities. I value building great ideas from the ground up, turning human insight into tangible returns, and making thoughtful UI decisions. My current tools of choice are Sketch, Illustrator, Invision, Flinto, Zepplin, Atom, pen, paper, napkins, whatever. I’m always interested in chatting about new ideas, so feel free to shoot me a note or follow me on Twitter. ✌🏾
